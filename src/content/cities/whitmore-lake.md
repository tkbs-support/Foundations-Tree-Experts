Whitmore Lake is a lakeside community spanning Northfield Township in Washtenaw County and Green Oak Township in Livingston County. The area is defined by Whitmore Lake itself, with waterfront properties, surrounding rural land, and the natural beauty that draws residents to this part of Southeast Michigan.

Foundations Tree Experts serves the Whitmore Lake area with tree removal, trimming, stump grinding, and emergency response. We're a short drive north of Ann Arbor and work in the area regularly.

## Tree care around Whitmore Lake

The Whitmore Lake area has distinct tree care needs:

- **Lakefront properties** — trees along the shoreline can threaten docks, seawalls, and views. Removal near water requires careful planning and sometimes specialized equipment access.
- **Rural lots** — many properties in the Whitmore Lake area sit on larger lots with significant tree cover, requiring periodic pruning, storm damage cleanup, or selective clearing.
- **Emerald ash borer** — Washtenaw County has been impacted by EAB since its discovery in 2002. Many properties still have standing dead ash.
- **Oak wilt risk** — present across Southeast Michigan. Oaks should not be pruned between April and July.
