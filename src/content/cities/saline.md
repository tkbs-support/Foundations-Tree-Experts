Saline sits just south of Ann Arbor — a smaller city with a strong community identity, excellent schools, and a landscape that blends established residential neighborhoods with agricultural land and newer development. It's the kind of place where a mature oak in the backyard is part of what makes a property feel like home.

Foundations Tree Experts serves Saline homeowners with the same standards and crew we bring to every job across Washtenaw County. We're just a short drive from Ann Arbor and work in Saline regularly.

## Neighborhoods we serve in Saline

We work throughout Saline and surrounding areas, including:

- **Brookview Highlands** — 119 custom homes on 1+ acre lots north of downtown, with significant tree cover on larger properties
- **Rolling Meadows** — 1960s-era ranch and two-story homes with mature trees from the original plantings
- **Silo Ridge** — active neighborhood community with well-maintained properties
- **Beckington** — east of Moon Road, 1970s homes on 1+ acre lots with established landscaping
- **Brecon Park** — residential area southeast of Brookview Highlands
- **York Meadows** — sought-after neighborhood with family-friendly character

## Tree challenges in Saline

Saline's mix of older neighborhoods and rural-suburban properties creates varied tree care needs:

- **Emerald ash borer** — Washtenaw County has been impacted by EAB since its discovery in Southeast Michigan in 2002. Many Saline properties still have dead or declining ash trees that need safe removal.
- **Oak wilt risk** — present in Southeast Michigan across 56 counties. Oaks should not be pruned between April 15 and July 15 to prevent the spread of this fungal disease.
- **Mature trees on older lots** — neighborhoods like Rolling Meadows (built in the 1960s) have trees that are now 60+ years old. These large, aging trees can develop structural problems and outgrow residential lots.
- **Rural-suburban properties** — larger lots outside of Saline's core neighborhoods often have significant tree cover that requires periodic maintenance, storm damage cleanup, or clearing for additions and outbuildings.

## Tree service for Saline's growing community

As Saline continues to grow with new residential development, homeowners need reliable tree service for both existing properties and new builds. Whether it's removing a storm-damaged tree from a Rolling Meadows backyard, pruning the mature oaks on a Brookview Highlands acre lot, or clearing a site for new construction, we bring the same professional approach to every job.
