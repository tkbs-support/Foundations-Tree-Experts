Foundations Tree Experts serves communities across western Oakland County, including Wixom, Milford, Walled Lake, and Commerce Township. This part of Oakland County features a mix of established lakefront communities, newer subdivisions, and semi-rural properties — all with tree care needs.

## Cities we serve

- **Wixom** (48393) — suburban community with a mix of residential and commercial properties
- **Milford** (48380, 48381) — charming village atmosphere with established tree canopy and surrounding rural properties
- **Walled Lake** (48390) — lakefront community with waterfront tree care needs
- **Commerce Township** (48382) — one of Oakland County's growing communities with wooded lots and newer development

## Common tree needs in Oakland County

- **Lakefront tree care** — Walled Lake and surrounding lake communities have trees that threaten docks, shorelines, and water access. Careful removal and pruning preserves both safety and lake views.
- **Emerald ash borer** — Oakland County has been heavily impacted by EAB. Dead and declining ash remain throughout these communities.
- **Wooded lot management** — many properties in Milford, Commerce Township, and surrounding areas sit on wooded lots that require periodic maintenance, hazard tree assessment, and selective removal.
- **New development** — growing communities need lot clearing and site prep for new construction.
- **Oak wilt risk** — present across Southeast Michigan. Oaks should not be pruned between April and July.

All services are available — tree removal, trimming & pruning, stump grinding, emergency storm response, crane-assisted removal, and lot clearing.
