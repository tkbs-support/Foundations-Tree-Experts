Belleville is a small city in Wayne County situated around Belleville Lake, with the surrounding Van Buren Township making up much of the broader community. The area blends lakefront living with suburban neighborhoods and rural properties — each with its own tree care needs.

Foundations Tree Experts serves Belleville and Van Buren Township with tree removal, trimming, stump grinding, and 24/7 emergency response.

## Tree care in Belleville

- **Lakefront properties** — Belleville Lake creates waterfront tree care needs, from large trees threatening docks to shade trees that need careful pruning to maintain both health and lake views.
- **Suburban neighborhoods** — established subdivisions have mature trees that may need trimming, removal, or stump grinding as they age.
- **Rural properties** — larger lots outside the city core have significant tree cover requiring periodic maintenance.
- **Emerald ash borer** — Wayne County has been heavily impacted by EAB since its discovery in 2002. Dead and declining ash remain on many properties.
- **Oak wilt risk** — present across Southeast Michigan. Oaks should not be pruned between April and July.
