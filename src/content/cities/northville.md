Northville is known for its walkable, tree-lined neighborhoods that reflect a strong sense of community and pride of place. From the Historic District within walking distance of downtown to the more spacious, country-style settings of surrounding areas, mature trees are part of what makes Northville feel like Northville.

Foundations Tree Experts serves Northville homeowners with tree removal, trimming, stump grinding, and 24/7 emergency storm response. We're nearby and work in the Northville area regularly.

## Neighborhoods we serve in Northville

We work throughout the City of Northville and Northville Township, including:

- **Historic District** — walkable downtown area with mature street trees and established landscaping
- **Beal Town** — one of Northville's distinct neighborhoods with character homes
- **Cabbagetown** — residential area with a mix of architectural styles and mature plantings
- Various subdivisions, condominiums, and country-style properties throughout the township

## Tree challenges in Northville

- **Emerald ash borer** — like all of Southeast Michigan, Northville has lost significant ash trees to EAB since its discovery in 2002. Dead and declining ash remain on many properties.
- **Mature trees on historic lots** — Northville's older neighborhoods have trees planted decades ago that can outgrow their lots, encroaching on rooflines and driveways.
- **Oak wilt risk** — oaks should not be pruned between April and July to prevent the spread of this fungal disease, which is present across Southeast Michigan.

## Local tree regulations

Both the City of Northville and Northville Township have tree-related ordinances:

- The City of Northville's Chapter 90 (Vegetation) covers tree care requirements
- Northville Township's tree and woodlands replacement ordinance protects existing trees in development areas, with trees within 10 feet of a building footprint exempt from preservation requirements

We recommend checking with your municipality before scheduling removal.
