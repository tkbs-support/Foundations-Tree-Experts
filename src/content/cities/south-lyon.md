South Lyon is a growing community in Oakland County where newer development meets established neighborhoods and surrounding rural land. The city's zoning ordinance emphasizes preserving natural features — including trees and significant vegetation — as properties develop and change.

Foundations Tree Experts serves South Lyon with tree removal, trimming, stump grinding, and emergency response. We're in the area regularly and treat every South Lyon job with the same standards as our work across Southeast Michigan.

## Tree care in South Lyon

South Lyon's landscape creates a mix of tree care needs:

- **Established neighborhoods** — older subdivisions have mature trees that may have outgrown their lots or developed structural issues over decades of growth.
- **New development areas** — as South Lyon grows, builders and homeowners need lot clearing and selective tree removal for new construction.
- **Rural-suburban transition** — properties on the edges of South Lyon often have significant tree cover on larger lots, requiring periodic maintenance or storm damage cleanup.
- **Emerald ash borer** — Oakland County has been impacted by EAB. Dead and declining ash trees remain on many properties.
- **Oak wilt risk** — present across Southeast Michigan. Oaks should not be pruned between April and July.
