## Novi's Woodland Protection Ordinance

Novi's ordinance (Chapter 37) is more detailed than most cities in the area. Key points homeowners should know:

- **Within regulated woodlands:** all trees 8 inches DBH (diameter at breast height) or greater are protected
- **Outside regulated woodlands:** trees 36 inches DBH or greater are protected
- **Historic or specimen trees** are also protected regardless of location
- **Replacement trees** must be native to Michigan species listed in Section 38
- The city maintains a **Regulated Woodland Map** — check whether your property falls within a regulated area before planning any tree work
