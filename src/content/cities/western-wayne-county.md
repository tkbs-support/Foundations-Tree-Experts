Foundations Tree Experts serves communities across western Wayne County, including Romulus, Taylor, Dearborn Heights, and Allen Park. These established suburban cities share common tree care needs — aging street trees, emerald ash borer impact, and storm damage from Southeast Michigan's seasonal weather.

## Cities we serve

- **Romulus** (48174) — residential and commercial properties near Detroit Metro Airport
- **Taylor** (48180) — one of Wayne County's larger cities with established residential neighborhoods
- **Dearborn Heights** (48125, 48127) — older suburban neighborhoods with mature tree canopy
- **Allen Park** (48101) — tree-lined residential streets with homes dating back decades

## Common tree needs in western Wayne County

- **Emerald ash borer cleanup** — Wayne County was ground zero for EAB's discovery in 2002. Many properties still have dead ash standing or declining ash that needs removal before it becomes a hazard.
- **Aging suburban trees** — these cities were largely built out in the mid-20th century, meaning street and yard trees are now 50-70+ years old and may need removal or significant pruning.
- **Storm damage** — Southeast Michigan's thunderstorms and ice storms regularly bring down limbs and whole trees. We respond 24/7.
- **Stump grinding** — after removal, we grind stumps below grade and backfill so the area is ready for reuse.

All services are available across these communities — tree removal, trimming & pruning, stump grinding, emergency storm response, crane-assisted removal, and lot clearing.
