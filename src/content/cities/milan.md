Milan is a small city in southern Washtenaw County (extending into Monroe County) with a mix of in-town residential streets and surrounding rural properties. The area's agricultural heritage means many lots have significant tree cover — from mature hardwoods on older properties to volunteer growth on former farmland.

Foundations Tree Experts serves Milan with tree removal, trimming, stump grinding, and emergency response. We're based in the Ann Arbor area and work in Milan regularly.

## Tree care in Milan

- **Rural properties** — larger lots outside Milan's downtown core often have extensive tree cover requiring periodic pruning, hazard tree removal, or lot clearing for improvements.
- **Emerald ash borer** — Washtenaw County has been impacted by EAB since 2002. Dead and declining ash remain on many properties.
- **Oak wilt risk** — present across Southeast Michigan. Oaks should not be pruned between April and July.
- **Storm damage** — rural and semi-rural properties are exposed to Southeast Michigan's seasonal storms. We respond 24/7.
