SMS Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 8, 2026

We, at Foundations Tree Experts, are committed to protecting your privacy. This SMS Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal information specifically related to our mobile messaging services.

1. Information We Collect

When you opt-in to receive SMS messages from us, we may collect:

  • Your mobile phone number.
  • Your name (if provided).
  • Any information you include in your message responses.
  • Message interaction data (e.g., timestamps, delivery status, and click-through rates).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide and improve our SMS services, including:

  • Sending transactional alerts (e.g., order updates, appointment reminders).
  • Responding to your customer support inquiries.
  • Analyzing message performance to enhance our communications.

3. No Sharing of SMS Opt-In Data

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

4. Your Choices and Opt-Out Rights

You have full control over your participation in our SMS programs:

  • Opt-Out: You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to the number you received the messages from. After you send "STOP," we will send one final message to confirm your unsubscribe status.
  • Help: For more information or assistance, text "HELP" to the same number or contact us at foundationstreeexperts@gmail.com.

5. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

6. Message Frequency and Rates

Message frequency varies based on the program you have joined. Standard message and data rates may apply for any messages sent to you from us and to us from you. If you have any questions about your text plan or data plan, please contact your wireless provider.

By providing your phone number, you agree to receive text messages from Foundations Tree Experts. This is considered opting in. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.
